# Z5 Capital — Social Media Post Design Guidelines

## Colors
- **Z5 Charcoal:** #212529
- **White:** #FFFFFF
- **Light Grey:** #F8F9FA
- **Grey (attribution):** #6C757D
- **Z5 Gradient:** Magenta (#E91E63) to Violet (#7B1FA2)

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat ExtraBold, ALL CAPS
- **Body:** Inter Regular, sentence case, max 20 words per slide
- **CTA:** Inter SemiBold
- **Alignment:** Always left-aligned

## Post Templates

### A. "Thought Leader" (Quote/Insight)
- Background: White #FFFFFF or light grey #F8F9FA
- Large oversized quotation mark in Z5 Gradient (top left)
- Quote: Montserrat Bold 60pt+, Z5 Charcoal
- Attribution: Inter Medium 30pt, Grey
- Footer: Small Z5 Logo centered bottom

### B. "Milestone" (Funding/New Hire)
- Split screen or card style
- Gradient border 4px (bottom or left edge)
- Headline: "Welcome to the Family: [Name]" Montserrat ExtraBold

### C. "Data Drop" (Charts/Trends)
- Background: Z5 Charcoal #212529
- Chart elements: Z5 Gradient (Magenta-to-Violet) — makes data pop like neon
- Text: White for legibility
- Faded Z5 Prism icon at 10% opacity in background
- Z5 Logo: Bottom right, small, unobtrusive

## Platform Specs
| Platform | Orientation | Focus |
|----------|------------|-------|
| LinkedIn | 4:5 Portrait or Square | Professional, Insight & Data templates |
| Twitter/X | 16:9 Landscape | Simple charts, single-sentence graphics |
| Instagram | 1:1 Square or 9:16 Stories | Culture, milestones, team highlights |

## Do Not
- No gradient on body text
- No more than one primary message per image
- Never stretch the logo
- Never put charcoal text on dark images
